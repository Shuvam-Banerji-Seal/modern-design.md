About

A creative developer based in Paris, focused on interactive 3D for the browser. The work leans on Three.js, custom shaders, and a healthy amount of physics.

This portfolio doubles as the demo: every region of the island reveals a different chapter of the work, the controls, the credits, and the small games tucked inside.

The build is hand-rolled — Vite, Three.js, Rapier for the vehicle, Howler.js for the audio. No framework, just classes and a single render loop.
